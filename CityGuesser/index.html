City Guesser Game

Instructions

Welcome!

Think you know lots of U.S. cities? Test your knowledge below to find out how much of the U.S. map you are able to fill in by naming cities!

For each city you name, all nearby cities within a particular radius are also revealed to you without having to name them directly. Scores are shown below the map that describe the number of cities that have been named, what proportion of U.S. cities have been revealed, and some other fun goals to aim for. When you loaded the page, the map should have automatically been set to an appropriate size. However, if you would prefer to change the size of the map, use the buttons above the map. This quiz includes the 48 contiguous states and D.C. totally to 49 "states."

Are you able to name 1000 citites? Cover each state at least once? Or do you think you can reveal all of the citites within the U.S.? The only way to proove yourself and find out is to scroll down and start naming cities. Have fun!
